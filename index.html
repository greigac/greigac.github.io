About Me

I started gaining an interest in technology at around the age of 13 where I started developing websites back in the days of Geocities where I created several sites and configuring message boards based off other hobbies of mine. I continued to study the ins and outs of how a pc functioned before deciding that computing was something I wanted to pursue as a career. I went on to attend the University of the Highlands and Islands where I completed my Bachelor of Science Honors Degree in Computing.

Throughout university I have learned a wide range of languages such as: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP while working with databases such as MySQL. I also learned the fundamentals of computers and network server management. I mainly worked on front-end development with a touch of back-end.

Since graduating I’ve continued to improve my skills and keep up to date with technology while learning new frameworks and languages. A portfolio that has several pieces of my work can be found on my github: https://github.com/greigac I am currently seeking to find work where I can put what I have learned to use.
